Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 June 2026 · Applies to Wibbly Works Inc and its games, including Betweenies.

The short version: we collect as little as possible. Our games work without an account, we don't sell data, and we don't show third-party ads.

What we collect

  • Optional display name. If you choose to join a leaderboard, the name you enter (which can be anything — it doesn't have to identify you) and your scores are stored so they can be shown on the leaderboard.
  • Gameplay data on your device. Progress, scores, and settings are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to us, except for leaderboard scores as described above.
  • Purchase records. If a game offers in-app purchases, purchases are processed by Apple. We (via our payments partner) receive an anonymised record of the purchase so we can unlock what you bought and restore it later — never your payment details.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell or share your personal information with data brokers or advertisers.
  • We don't show third-party advertising or use cross-app tracking.
  • We don't require an account or an email address to play.

Service providers

We use a small number of infrastructure providers to run our games: Supabase (leaderboard storage), RevenueCat (in-app purchase management), and Apple (app distribution and payments). Each receives only the data needed to do its job.

Children

Our games are designed to be suitable for all ages. We don't knowingly collect personal information from children; leaderboard names are optional and free-text, and we encourage younger players not to use their real names.

Your choices

You can play without ever submitting a leaderboard score. To ask us to remove a leaderboard entry or for any other privacy request, email hello@wibbly.works and we'll sort it out.

Changes

If we change this policy we'll update it here and adjust the date at the top.
