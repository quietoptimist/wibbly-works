Betweenies

Out now on iPhone

The word-betweening puzzle. Place the missing middle words to link pairs together — take it slow in Chill mode, or beat the clock in Race mode and challenge your friends to top your time.

Download on the App Store betweenies.app

SlashCart

Free on the web

Your grocery list. Best unit prices. Cart built. Done. Paste a list or snap a receipt — SlashCart finds the best real cost per unit across Amazon and Walmart, then builds your cart for you. Not a price comparison tool: a buying tool.
